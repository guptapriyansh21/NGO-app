Our Mission

Education gives us the ability to differentiate between what is right and what is wrong, what is immoral and moral & what is just and unjust. The main aim of our NGO is to bestow the light of primary and secondary education on the underprivileged children. Alongwith imparting education , our motive is the overall development of children as an individual to make them self-dependent & competent. Education helps us learn the right way to execute oneself and behave properly.

Our Founder





"The happiest people we know are those who lose themselves in the service of others."

- Gordon B. Hinckley

So is our founder Mr. Siddharth Wadhwani. An ambitious, compassionate, diligent and a rational personality.

His dedication towards social service and affection for children is exorbitant.

He holds an honours degree in electronics & communication.

From the beginning , Mr. Siddharth has an inclination towards the upliftment of society and its betterment.

He possess excellent leadership skills which are quite evident by the fact that he is currently leading three chapters of Ummeedon ko pankh. He started with the Gorakhpur chapter followed by the Ghaziabad and Greater Noida Chapters.

Moreover, he also plans to create a group of engineers which would not only educate kids but can also solve the day to day problems in slums with the help of technology.
